Philip and Keegan Brown are on a quest to visit every county seat in Indiana. Several times each month we will go out and vitit a few counties, stopping to take pictures and look around. We've been through weather ranging from -2.1°F to 102.2°F, and wind enough to blow the top of my hamburger off. This site shows what we have found in each county, including a brief history of the county and courthouse and a picture of each. Also, it shows which counties have been visited (those in yellow on the map), and which ones are left.
