The Solution


 

   Providing access to computers for everyone in our society is the only solution. The simplest and most effective way to do this is to establish community computing centers to meet the computing needs of a general community, especially those who would otherwise have no access. This would be easily accomplished by putting a staff into a school or computer room, with little overhead cost because the computers have already been paid for by the taxpayers. Such a solution serves to educate the public about computers as well as training them on how to use a computer.
