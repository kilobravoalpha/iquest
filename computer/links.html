Community Networks


Austin Free-Net

   The Austin Free-Net is a community sponsored program to put computers into public places in Austin. Their goal is to provide computer access to all, especially those who would otherwise have no computer access. This provides an excellent example of a solution similar to mane at work.

Indianapolis Online

   Indianapolis Online is a non-profit community network established to provide community information to the general public of Indianapolis.

Seattle Community Network

   The Seattle Community Network is a volunteer-operated public-access computer network dedicated to providing equal access to information for all users.


Organizations


Alliance for Public Technology

   The Alliance for Public Technology is a non-profit organization helping to distribute information in a cost-free manner to all citizens.

Organization for Community Networks

   The Organization for Community Networks is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing information to those who wish to establish and maintain community networks and free-nets.
